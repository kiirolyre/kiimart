Market Mission

KiiMart aims to create a welcoming space where artists can connect, share their work, and have fun! This market has FREE public entry for everyone, accessible layouts, and is inspired by early-2000s digital design... Think Miiverse and Nintendo ;〕! We embrace under-the-radar creators bringing new perspectives, so you'll find handmade treasures you won’t get anywhere else.
