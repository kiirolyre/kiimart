Our Mission

Kiimart is here to support creatives who are just starting out — whether you’re tabling for the first time or fitting your art around work or study. We prioritize original, handmade works and community-driven vibes.

Market Vision

We aim to create a relaxed and welcoming event with free public entry, accessible venue layouts, and a focus on nostalgic, playful aesthetics. Think beaded trinkets, zines, textiles, stickers, keychains and more!
